US Visa Application Guide

Planning to travel to the United States? Here's your complete guide to applying for a US visitor visa (B-1/B-2) from India.

📋 Step-by-Step Application Process

1. Determine Your Visa Type

B-1 Visa: Business purposes, conferences, meetings
B-2 Visa: Tourism, vacation, visiting family/friends
B-1/B-2: Combined business and tourism

2. Complete DS-160 Form

• Fill out the Online Nonimmigrant Visa Application (DS-160)
• Upload a digital photo meeting US visa requirements
• Print the confirmation page with barcode

3. Pay Visa Fees

• Visa application fee: $185 USD
• Pay online or at designated banks
• Keep the receipt for your records

4. Schedule Interview

• Book appointment at US Consulate/Embassy
• Available locations: New Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, Kolkata, Hyderabad
• Wait times vary by location and season

5. Prepare Required Documents

Essential Documents:
• Valid passport (6+ months validity)
• DS-160 confirmation page
• Visa fee payment receipt
• Recent photograph
• Interview appointment confirmation

Supporting Documents:
• Bank statements (last 3-6 months)
• Employment letter and salary slips
• Property documents
• Travel itinerary
• Hotel bookings
• Invitation letter (if visiting someone)
• Previous travel history

6. Attend Visa Interview

• Arrive 15 minutes early
• Dress professionally
• Answer questions honestly and confidently
• Demonstrate strong ties to India
• Show financial stability

💡 Pro Tips for Success

Strong Ties to India: Demonstrate employment, property ownership, family relationships, or other compelling reasons to return to India.

Financial Proof: Show sufficient funds to cover your trip expenses. Bank balance should ideally be 3-4 times your trip cost.

Travel History: Previous international travel (especially to developed countries) strengthens your application.

Interview Preparation: Practice common questions about your travel purpose, duration, and ties to India.

⏰ Processing Times

Interview Wait Time: 2-8 weeks (varies by location)
Processing After Interview: 1-3 working days
Passport Return: 2-5 working days

💰 Current Fees (2025)

Visa Application Fee: $185 USD (₹15,500 approx.)
SEVIS Fee: Not applicable for B-1/B-2
Additional Costs: Photos, document translation, travel to consulate
